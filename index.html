Autarkies

Autarkies for SAT are partial assignments for boolean CNF, which either satisfy a clause or leave it untouched.

A- and E-systems

Consider a DQCNF F and k ≥ 0:
– An A k-autarky for F is an autarky such that all boolean functions assigned depend essentially on at most k variables.
– An E k -autarky is an autarky assigns at most k (existential) variables.
